Website Needs
The most essential elements for a successful nonprofit website are the ones listed below including
 responsiveness, efficient donation system, timely support etc.
Online Donation System
Manage currencies, donation packages, donors and payment gateways through our Lifeline Donation Pro plugin to collection donation anywhere in the world.

Get Recurring Donations
Create Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly or Yearly period for recurring donations and the donations will be charged on specific time automatically and an email will be sent to the admin and the donor.
Manage Events
Manage past or upcoming events with a custom post type. We have given a complete event management system completely customizable through Elementor.

Perfectly Responsive
The home pages and inner pages of Lifeline WP theme are perfectly responsive keeping in mind the sharp increase of smart phone users in 2021 and 2024.

WooCommerce To Sell Online
Raise funds for your nonprofit organization by selling products online through WooCommerce plugin. Our themes is fully compatible with this marvellous WP plugin
Cause Management
Create causes for raising funds easily through custom post type and set the funds limit needed for a specific cause in the backend and start raising donations.

Easy Demo Installation
Use our 1-Click demo install system to import the example data. Then you can change the data and pictures according to your needs to make it your own.

Modern Clean Graphics
Visually appealing typography and clear design makes it a clean WordPress theme with distinguished sections. Everything can be personalized with Elementor page builder.
Personalize Anything
Create sidebars, make new pages, manage the team, personlize about us page or do literally any change to adapt this theme to your needs with complete backend control.
